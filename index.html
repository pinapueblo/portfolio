Section 01 — About

Human Spec Sheet.

Fig. 1 — Me!

Hi! I'm Pablo, an undergraduate Mechanical Engineering student with a minor in Robotics at The University of Texas at Austin. I'm interested in the intersection of mechanical design, automation, and embedded systems, with a focus on building systems that tightly integrate hardware and software. I enjoy transforming concepts into physical builds, whether that means designing components in CAD, prototyping through additive and subtractive manufacturing, or programming microcontrollers and robotic systems. My projects have given me hands-on experience across the full development process, from modeling and analysis to fabrication and testing.

In addition to engineering, I work as a private tutor for Algebra II, SAT preparation, and Statics. Teaching has strengthened my ability to break down complex concepts and communicate technical ideas clearly — skills I love bringing into team-based engineering environments. Outside of engineering, I enjoy working out, learning new languages, and exploring mathematics.
