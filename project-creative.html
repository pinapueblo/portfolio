Design Philosophy

  • Combine engineering principles with industrial design.
  • Prototype ideas rapidly through CAD and additive manufacturing.
  • Apply woodworking and finishing techniques to produce polished products.
  • Balance aesthetics, durability, and functionality.
  • Continuously iterate designs based on fabrication experience.
